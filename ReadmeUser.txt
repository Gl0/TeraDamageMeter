Tera Damage Meter by Gothos

* Make sure you have installed .NET 4.0 or later
*   Install npcap from https://github.com/nmap/npcap/releases
  OR 
    Add firewall exception, allowing all incoming connetions for the meter 
    and enable raw sockets mode in settings (may need restart).
* Launch the Damage Meter before connecting to Tera, or it won't detect the connection.
